## Account Registration & Login 

SecAI provides multiple ways for users to register and sign in.Users can choose between **third-party login** or **manual registration**:

<img src="/images/account01.png" alt="" style={{margin: '0 auto', maxWidth: '100%'}} />

## Third-Party Login 

- Click **“Continue with Google”** or **“Continue with GitHub”**.
- Authenticate with your third-party account.
- You will be redirected to SecAI and automatically signed in.

## Manual Registration/Login

- Click **“Create an Account”**.
- Enter your **email, password**, and confirm the password.
- Click **"Sign Up"**, then check your email for a **verification link**.
- Click the link to verify your email.
- You will be **automatically logged into SecAI**.

## Login for Existing Users

- If you already have an account, enter your **email and password** on the login page.
- If you forgot your password, click **"Forgot Password?"** to reset it.

## Usage Limits & Query Restrictions

SecAI enforces different limits based on whether a user is logged in and their account plan. Query limits apply to both Research & Chat modules.

<img src="/images/account02.png" alt="" style={{margin: '0 auto', maxWidth: '100%'}} />

<img src="/images/account03.png" alt="" style={{margin: '0 auto', maxWidth: '100%', marginTop: '20px'}} />

|User Type|Query Limit|Reset Time|
|---|---|---|
|Guest (No Login)|	5 queries per day	 |Daily at 00:00 UTC|
|Basic Account|	10 queries per 4 hours	|Every 4 hours from 00:00 UTC|
|Pro Account|	100 queries per 4 hours	|Every 4 hours from 00:00 UTC|

## Upgrading to a Pro Account

Users who need higher query limits can redeem an invite code to upgrade to a Pro account.

<img src="/images/account04.png" alt="" style={{margin: '0 auto', maxWidth: '100%'}} />

1. Click on the **profile avatar** in the bottom-left corner of the page.
2. Click **“Upgrade”** in the menu.Enter your **invite code**.
3. Click **“Redeem”** to activate **Pro-tier benefits**.

## Pro Benefits
**✅100 queries every 4 hours**
